Class: Wheel Basics

Tutor: Lauren Steward
Level: 0/5
Availability: 25
Cost: $35/person

Learn the basic techniques to throw cylinders, cups and bowls in this beginners class. A relaxed and casual class, and the perfect introduction to the pottery industry. By the end of the workshop you will choose 2 favourite items to be bisc fired, and you will learn how to apply glaze to finish your pieces, ready for their final glaze firing.

Clay and tools are included in the course cost.

Class: Form and Function on the Wheel

Tutor: Tracey Leoung
Level: 2/5
Availability: 15
Cost: $50/person

This intermediate throwing class is open to all who have previously experienced beginner throwing.

In this class Tracey will teach you how to throw fundamental tableware shapes including large plates, bowls and bottles and will focus on centring larger amounts of clay. The intention of this class is to expand your throwing abilities, technique, knowledge of form and function and to complete a single or double person dinner set.

Class: Form and Function on the Wheel

Tutor: Rachel Peterson
Level: 3/5
Availability: 15
Cost: $60/person

Potter Rachel Peterson creates functional porcelain domestic ware on the wheel. This is the perfect class for more experienced throwers.

In this throwing class Rachel will focus on design and will get you started thinking about how your pieces work together and how to make a functional piece also look good. The skills covered will be those extra bits that give you the ability to demonstrate your individual style eg handles, spouts, lids. The goal of this workshop will be to come away with a set where the cup, bowl and plate are all tied together with their design language.

Clay and tools are included for this class, however you can bring your own materials and tools..
